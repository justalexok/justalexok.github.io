What does the future of AI videogames look like

The future of videogames is changing. With the development of advancements in AI, the possibilities for the game industry are growing incredibly. AI can create more complex game experiences that are impossible using traditional programming. It can dynamically create levels or responses based on player input. It can even create intuitive NPCs that interact differently to each person who plays the game.

Not only will AI make gameplay more enjoyable, but it will also make online gaming more competitive. AI gamers can learn from player input and improve their skill over time, pushing the narrative of online gaming forward.

We are also seeing games becoming more social. Instead of just playing single-player AI games, gamers can now join up and play against opponents in a variety of social environments. Not only is this great for game development, but it's great for gamers too, as it connects them with others and exposes them to fresh experiences.
